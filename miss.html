Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children trilogy by Ransom Riggs

I read the Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children trilogy by Ransom Riggs. I really liked the plot, which was very unorthodox and is not at all like the average fantasy book ones. Although the three books are quite lengthy, the read was very enjoyable an there wasn't a dull moment. I also noticed how well Riggs developed all of the characters, making the reader fond of every one by the end of the series. My favourite book of the three was definitely Library of Souls, the third and final book, since it is where all the pieces in the puzzle come together. Also, having watched the film as well, I would like to add that the books are by far better!
