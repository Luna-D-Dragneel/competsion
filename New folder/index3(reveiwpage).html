Fantastic Beasts And Where To Find Them The Original Screenplay

I love this book, this is the screenplay so it was written like a script and because of that I managed to get a clearer image of the movie when I imagined it in my head. It tells the story bit by bit as expected from the author J.K Rowling. She is great at what she does, and I am glad she does it. Usually when we think of heroes from the school of Hogwarts we think of Gryffindor or sometimes we this of Ravenclaw and a bad house to be in we thing Hufflepuff because they get picked on or are the week ones, Slytherin is the house where, it is most known for its secrets.

The protagonist, Newt, is from Hufflepuff here, Newt shows great care for all the beasts alike, does not take notice of others like him. He cared for their wellbeing and nothing else. This shows how far his kind heart takes him, for beast to people to other things even more dangers then you could think of. This shows us more about the house we do not see in the Harry Potter.
